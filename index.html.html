About Me

Designing with Purpose & Passion

Hi, I'm Kartikeya — a Graphic Designer and 3D Environment Artist with a sharp eye for detail and a love for transforming ideas into impactful visuals. Based in Jaipur, India, I work at Pure Cosmeceuticals Pvt. Ltd. and take on freelance projects worldwide.

My work spans two creative worlds: polished 2D design in branding, social media and print — and immersive 3D environments built for games and digital experiences. I bridge both disciplines to bring complete creative vision to every project.
